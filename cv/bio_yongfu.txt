Yibo Lin received the B.S. degree in microelectronics from Shanghai Jiaotong University, Shanghai, China, in 2013. 
He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Texas at Austin, under supervision of Professor David Z. Pan. 
His research interests include physical design and design for manufacturability.

He has received Franco Cerrina Memorial Best Student Paper Award at SPIE Advanced Lithography Conference 2016, 
and National Scholarship at Shanghai Jiaotong University in 2012. 
He has interned at IMEC, Cadence, and Oracle.
